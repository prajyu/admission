Thrissur

Government Engineering College Thrissur (GEC Thrissur) formerly Government Engineering College Trichur, an ISO 9001: 2008 certified institution is one of the most prestigious and reputed technical education institution in south India. The College was established in the year 1957 with under graduate programmes in Civil, Mechanical and Electrical engineering branches. The foundation stone of the college Main Building was laid down by the then Hon. Prime Minister of India, Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru. Prof. S. Rajaraman, a Civil Engineer of international repute and author of several books in engineering was the first Principal of the college.

How to apply for

Allotment

KEAM Seat Allotment will be through the counselling process that begins from October 2021 onwards. The seat is allotted through Centralized Admission Process (CAP), a Single Window System (SWS). In this system, the authorites allot the seats to the candidates in colleges and courses through online submission of options. Therefore, it is mandatory for all candidates to fill the options during KEAM 2021 Counselling according to their preference, i.e. colleges and courses they are sure to join upon allotment. The admission to the engineering courses is done based on the rank list published. Merit is finalized by giving equal weightage of 50:50 to the marks secured in both papers of KEAM 2021 ie marks secured in ( Physics and Chemistry) (Maths) together.

An allotment memo is generated for the selected candidates. It contains details such as name, roll number, allotted course & college, category of allotment and fee details among others. The fee must be paid at the branches of State Bank of India. An option to make an online payment on the prescribed date is also available. College Union

Programs

The college union 2019-20 was elected on 4th September 2019, with Mohammed Arshad P as Chairperson and Adhil Falah as General Secretary. This report covers the numerous activities conducted by this college union. Bhoomika'19, the official inauguration of the newly elected union was done on October 2 by the college principal Dr. Sheeba V S along with the film crew of 'Edakkad Battalion 06' which included renowned artists like Tovino Thomas, Samyuktha Menon, Sankar Induchoodan and Nithya Mammen. As part of Gandhi Jayanthi, a campus cleaning activity in association with the college NSS unit, was also carried out. Then a cultural night with various stage performances of the students gave a colourful end to Bhoomika '19 and a good start to the union activities.

Union Athletics

A House wise competition with all the students teamed into four houses, competed to prove their athletic skills and excellent sportspersonship in the Union athletics conducted on 11th and 12th of October.

Nilavum Chiraathum

An interactive session with Anwar Ali followed by the official inauguration of magazine committee was conducted on 15th October. Stand up poetry was also conducted and the program marks an unforgettable experience for all GECians.

Inter department games

In the inter departmental games - Dunk, Ace, Hits and Golazo held from 23rd October to 6th November, teams from different departments made an effort to prove their mettle on the grounds of GEC

Diwali '19

Celebrating Diwali night lighting diyas and flying lanterns along with the music performance of Bansuri band, was conducted on 28th October and made a beautiful experience for GECians.

Akrithi

An ice breaking session for the first years including various sports, cultural and literary events, was conducted between November 11 and 15. It hosted a platform for the first years to showcase their talents and all of them participated with enthusiasm. The event concluded with Akrithi night with live performances by DJ Peter and Samuraizz.

Chithram '20

The film fest of GEC was conducted from January 27 to 29 screening short films, documentaries and movies of different genres including a poll movie on the final night. The quality of films and the theatre - like atmosphere brought out a mesmerizing treat to the eyes of massive GEC audience

GPL 20

GEC's premier cricket league GPL was officially inaugurated on 15 January. The league saw the participation of 8 teams which gained their icon players through auction contesting for the coveted trophy. The finals was played between Mekava CC and Kunzima CC on 4 February. Mekava won the match by 35 runs to be crowned champions.

Dyuthi cricket

The cricket league for women was held in February. As a pre - Dyuthi event with 6 women teams competing for the trophy, it proved an encouraging event for the lady cricketers of GEC

Dyuthi '20

The national level multi-fest, the biggest techno-cultural extravaganza of GEC, aimed at revealing the technical and cultural skills, was conducted from February 20 to 23.Dyuthi has always been in limelight for the social awareness campaigns associated with it. A social awareness campaign on the LGBTQI+ communities and Dyuthi Run - an endeavour to spread the message was conducted at Thrissur Swaraj round. As part of the campaign a talk session carried out by three LGBTQI+ organisation activists and a street play at Thekkinkad maidan, were conducted on the first day. GEC witnessed 3 days and 4 nights packed with multitude of techno-cultural and informal events. Dyuthi attracted thousands of students from several colleges with its fascinating shows by artists like Sooraj Santhosh, Anne Amie, Maari Ferrari and music band Lagori.

Kaithaang

The challenges that first year students usually face in academics is not small and keeping this in mind, SFI GEC launched a YouTube channel called "Kaithaang". This initiative aims at providing video lectures for all subjects for first year students. The channel later evolved into an app. With the latest revision of the KTU syllabus looming over them, notes for all subjects are provided through the app for all to access. Adding to this, the app has previous year question papers as well. These timely lectures are a huge relief for students to catch up on missed classes and difficult topics. In contrast to colleges that still have the outdated tradition of ragging, Kaithaang brings forward senior students eager to help and tutor their juniors.

Download kaithang app from here.



Padheyam

Commemorating the death of a young man named Madhu, who was the victim of an inhumane mass trial for alleged theft, SFI GEC Unit continues its noble initiative of providing meals for the not-so-fortunate sections of our society, on the 22nd of every month, unabated even during this covid crisis. Madhu paid the price of hunger with his life. Providing for the marginalised and the less fortunate is something we take huge pride in and on the 22nd of each month we set out to prove that humanity still exists.

WOW GEC

Women of Wisdom (WOW) is a platform that empowers the women of GEC and encourage them to step forward with their ideas. This is organised by Mathrukam GEC unit in collaboration with the college union. WOW GEC addresses the issues faced by women and the gender issues that still prevail in the society. With various initiatives and events, this platform has always raised its strong voice against the heinous activities and problems that exist around us. WOW GEC has voiced the experiences of countless GECians, and with ventures like the 'No-Go zone' ( Sex Education series) they have broken the walls of ignorance and stereotypes that surround us.

Red Ribbon Club

What more can be said about the importance of donating blood that hasn't yet been said! Our college has been the go-to spot for all hospitals and blood banks in and around Thrissur. Every GECian knows the importance of giving blood and saving lives. Our people here are ready to be called upon at any time, be it day or night. After all, blood is meant to be circulated, pass it on!

GEC Commune

Sitting at home and doing nothing during this lockdown period? SFI GEC had the solution for that too! In a growing tech-savvy world, our knowledge shouldn't be limited to what they teach in the classrooms. With this in mind, the initiative of GEC Commune was launched in a bid to provide students with an opportunity to water their passions by working from home in various fields and earning a few bucks along the way too!
